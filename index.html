Our Story

A tumbler of tea, poured the same way for over twenty years.

Selvaraj Tea Stall has been a fixture of Dindugul Road since 2002 — a small kadai with a big following, where the tea is brewed strong, the vadai is fried fresh, and everyone is on first-name terms with the regulars.

No shortcuts, no artificial flavouring — just leaves, milk, and the right amount of patience at the boil.

— Live, laugh, love, over a hot cup of tea.
